Kendo



One of my interests is Kendo, or Japanese Fencing. I practice it here at the OSU Kendo Club-- the first image is our logo. The second image is me during sparring practice.

There are four main targets in kendo:

  1. The "Men" or the Head
  2. The "Kote" or the Wrists
  3. The "Dou" or the Sides of the Body
  4. The "Tsuki" or the Throat
